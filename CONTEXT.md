## Context
This project is based on the XP Cyber Lab *"Preventative Protection: Thwarting the Imminent Threat"*.

In this scenario, the cybersecurity team of an unnamed company receives a tip that they are going to be attacked soon.
It is assumed that the attack in question will mostly revolve around the EternalBlue exploit. 
The goal is to secure affected machines as soon as possible in order to prevent the attack.
